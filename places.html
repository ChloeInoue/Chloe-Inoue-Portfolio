Memory

Tokyo & Kamakura, Japan

This last Spring my husband and I traveled to Tokyo to visit family. When we talk about this trip we often talk about the many miles that we walked each day and the food that we ate. I remember small things. I remember the way it would lightly rain in the morning. The late night trips to the convenience store around the corner (a plastic bag, onigiri, sake). The day we bought flowers from the grocery store, to decorate Masako's dining table. The day we made our wedding rings in Kamakura, and explored the shrines and the Hokokuji forest. Matcha, so bitter-- served with a tiny sugar cookie. These three photos perfectly capture this time:

  • the rainy days
  • the late night trips to convenience stores
  • buying flowers for the family dining table
  • visiting the shrines in Kamakura
